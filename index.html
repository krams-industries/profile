Pensio — Pensions Made Easy

GovTech

My thesis partner built an R-based pension model for the Bank of Latvia, which the central bank still uses today. We used it in our thesis and saw how opaque pension systems really are. Small mistakes in fund choice and fees compound into billions in lost retirement value. Pensio is my attempt to build a transparency layer for retirement outcomes.

Silent Shepherd — Acoustic Monitoring for Industrial 3D Printing

AI / Industrial IoT

We identified a simple but expensive problem: industrial 3D print failures are often heard before they are seen. A clog or misfeed can quietly ruin a 14-hour print, waste material, and trigger costly downtime. Silent Shepherd turns audio into an early warning layer for machines.
